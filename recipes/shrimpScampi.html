Shrimp Scampi Recipe

Shrimp Scampi with Pasta

Shrimp scampi with linguine is the ultimate seafood pasta dish. Works with any kind of pasta; angel hair is less filling.

Ingredients Needed

  • Shrimp
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Shallots and garlic
  • White whine
  • Lemmon juice
  • Seasonings
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Boil linguine for 6 to 8 minutes
  3. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter with 2 tablespoons olive oil in large skillet over medium heat
  4. Cook and stir shallots, garlic, and red pepper in the butter until shallots are translucent, 3 to 4 minutes
  5. Season shrimp and cook in skillet 2 to 3 minutes
  6. Pour white whine and lemmon juice into skillet and bring to a boil
  7. Add butter and olive oil to skillet and bring to a simmer
  8. Toss linguine, shrimp, and parsley in the butter mixture until coated; season with salt and pepper. Drizzle with olive oil.
  9. Serve
