Chicken Parmesan Recipe

How To make Chicken Parmesan

This version is a little different than the way they do it in the restaurants, with less sauce and a crispier crust.

Ingredients Needed

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella, cut into small cubes
  • 1/4 cup chopped basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese

Steps

  1. Pound the chicken to an even thickness
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper
  3. Add parmesan to the bread crumbs and bread the chicken
  4. Let rest 15 minutes before cooking
  5. Preheat oven to 450o and cook for 20 minutes
