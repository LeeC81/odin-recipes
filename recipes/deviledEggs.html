Deviled Eggs

Deviled eggs are delicious!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonaise
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Vegetables
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Peel hard boild eggs
  2. Slice eggs in half length wise and remove yolks
  3. Mash yolks in a bowl. Stir in mayonaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, onion, and celery; mix well and season with salt
  4. Fill egg whites with yolk mixture
  5. Sprinkle with paprika and serve
